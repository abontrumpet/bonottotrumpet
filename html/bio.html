BIOGRAPHY

Alessandro Bonotto is a musician with the United States Naval Forces Europe Band and in the final stages of his doctoral degree at Michigan State University.

Prior to joining the US Navy Fleet Bands, Alessandro was the adjunct professor of trumpet at Saginaw Valley State University, the trumpet instructor at Flint Institute of Music, and maintained a private studio in the Greater Lansing area. As an active educator, he frequently gave masterclasses at local middle and high schools as well as at institutions such as University of Akron, Youngstown State University, Michigan State University, Montclair State University, The Juilliard School, and University of Wisconsin-Madison, as part of the faculty Beaumont Brass Quintet (MSU).

During his doctoral studies, Alessandro was the graduate teaching assistant at Michigan State University and the trumpet graduate assistant at Indiana University. At MSU his duties included playing with the faculty brass quintet, teaching lessons to undergraduates, as well as teaching trumpet methods to education majors. At IU he was the trumpet graduate assistant where he played in the associate-instructor quintet and assisted his professor, John Rommel. While there, he gave numerous world premieres for student composers, performed in the New Music Ensemble under David Dzubay, and was in the conductorâ€™s orchestra. For his Masters, Alessandro attended University of Oklahoma with Karl Sievers and for his undergraduate degree he attended University of Central Florida with John Almeida.

Alessandro has enjoyed a varied performing career mainly as an orchestral musician. In the summer of 2018, he attended the Aspen Music Festival where his primary teachers were Ray Mase, Kevin Cobb, and Jim Wilt. In years prior, he attended Brevard Music Center as a teaching assistant, Marrowstone Music Festival as a fellow, and Domain Forget where he was awarded a named scholarship. Alessandro performed frequently with Fort Wayne Philharmonic, Flint Symphony, Oklahoma City Philharmonic, and various others. Other performing engagements have included part of the national tour of Chicago The Musical, the Brass Band of Central Florida, and as a Sea World performing musician

Alessandro enjoys cycling around the greater Napoli area, trying different Neapolitan pizza places, and travelling with his wife, Anastasia.
